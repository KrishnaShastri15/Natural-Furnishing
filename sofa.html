Types of Sofa

Loveseats

Loveseats are smaller than regular sofas, typically offering seating space for two adults.

01.Modular Sofa

Modular sofas are made up of portable sections called modules.

This gives you the versatility to arrange your sofa in a range of different configurations to best suit your lifestyle and the layout of your space.

02.Arm Chair

An armchair is a big comfortable chair which has a support on each side for your arms.

03.Corner Sofa

a sofa that has a corner partway along it, so some of the seats are at right angles to the others.
